& Function

The concatenate function puts two strings together.

Syntax

expression1 & expression2

  • expression1 and expression2 can be any expression that rerults in a string. Note that spaces are required before and after the & symbol.

Example

Sample Data:
A = 4
B = 2.25

Formula
Result
1
 A & B 42.25
2
 A & " " & B 4 2.25
4
 fish & " " & out & " " & of & " " & water fish out of water
