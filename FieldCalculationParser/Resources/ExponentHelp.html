^ Function

The exponent operator (^) combines two numerical expressions through raising the first expression to the power of the second expression, resulting in a numerical result.

Syntax

expression1^expression2
  • expression1 and expression2 must be expressions that produce numerical results. Note that there are no spaces before or after the exponent operator.

Example


 Formula
Result
1
 3^2 9
2
 4.25^3.5 158.256390145778
